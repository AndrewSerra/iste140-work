About

I'm from a city in Turkey where there a lot of foreign families who moved there during the world wars. That shaped me as I grew up in a multicultural environment. My family is one of these families, and we are actually from Florence, Italy.

I went to highschool in Turkey and moved to Eindhoven, Netherlands to study Psychology and Technology with a focus of robotics. After studying for a year, I decided to come to the US and start studying at RIT. Upon graduation, I will be working as a Software Engineer, very likely in London, England. I love psychology and experience design, which I use when I'm coding frontend interfaces. My favorite researcher is Jane McGonigal from the Institute of the Future.
